RETURNS & EXCHANGE

Fresh product(s) can be exchanged from any of our retail stores within the 7 days of the date of delivery.Please note
that the product must be unused,all tags must be intact,packaging is in its original condition and origional invoice is.
Items bought on sale cannot be exchanged.Due to the differences in color monitors/screens & lighting used
during photoshoots,the exact color or texture of the final items may slightly vary from what is so on screen,
therefore products cannot be exchanged for this reason.we will only exchange or refund any product(s)if it is
defective or wrong product(s) has been delivered.

RREFUNDS

We have a No Refund Policy.However,refunds may be allowed in ecxceptional cases wherein the product is out of
stock.Refunds will be issued to the same credit card that we use for the origional purchase.You will be credit for
that cost of item(s) & applicable taxes.Shipping & handing costs are non-refundable.Please allow 7 days for the processing
of returns & up to two billing cycles for the credit to appear on your credit cards statement.

ORDER CANCELLATION

You may cancel your order any time before the order is processed.Once the order is shipped,you will receive,via
emil,a shipping confirmation that contains tracking information.Once a product is shipped,the 'Return & Exchange Policy'
will apply.Khaadi may cancle orders for any reason.Common reasons may include:the item is out of stock;pricing errors or
credit card payment is declined by the issuing financial institution.
